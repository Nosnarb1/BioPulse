Signal architecture

From raw motion to a live, interpretable muscle map.

The BioPulse array listens to the internal mechanics of movement. PVDF films capture micro-acoustic signatures from muscle fibers, IMUs track joint dynamics, and ultrasound imaging extracts structural behavior. These synchronized channels are reconstructed into a functional map of muscle activity.

This evolving map updates in real time and can be streamed into dashboards, robotics controllers, digital twins, and simulation systems. The same signal layer adapts across domains—human performance, rehabilitation, autonomous systems, and synthetic motion.
